Locate Request Tracking

Green indicates complete.  Pink indicates further activity is required before the Locate Package can be returned.

Ontario statutes require that a Locate Request be put in for every dig in Ontario. The complete Locate Package must be available on the job site should a One Call inspector show up.
Hover the mouse over Station Codes to see the full company name. Click the "Location" link to see the location of the dig in Google Maps.
You can bookmark a page that includes your search terms once you have done a search. Saving a bookmark with your name for search terms may be handy.
For "Standard" locates, members have 5 business days (typically 1 calendar week) to respond the these requests but many are often much quicker.
For "Advanced" locates, members have 10 business days (typically 2 calendar week) to respond the these requests but many are often much quicker.
